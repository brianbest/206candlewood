Dear Residents,

Welcome to 206 Candlewood Cres! We are pleased to launch this website as your go-to resource for building news, notices, and community updates.

## Our New Community Website

This site will serve as the primary channel for official communications from building management. You can expect to find:

- **Newsletters** — posted here as they are distributed
- **Important notices** — maintenance schedules, fire safety, and building updates
- **Seasonal reminders** — garbage and recycling schedules, parking policies, and more

We encourage you to bookmark this page and check back regularly.

## Building Management Contact

If you have questions or concerns, please contact building management through the usual channels. For emergencies, always call 911 first.

## Spring Reminders

As the warmer months approach, a few reminders for all residents:

- **Balconies** — please ensure balconies are kept tidy and free of items that could become projectiles in wind
- **Parking** — visitor parking is for visitors only; overnight guest parking requires a permit
- **Common areas** — please respect shared spaces and dispose of waste in the appropriate bins

We are glad to have you as part of our community at 206 Candlewood Cres.

Warmly,

**206 Candlewood Cres Building Management**
