Dear Residents,

Please be advised of upcoming maintenance work scheduled for the building. We appreciate your patience as we work to keep 206 Candlewood Cres in excellent condition.

**Dates:** Monday, April 28 – Friday, May 2, 2026

**Work areas:**

- **Parking garage** — line repainting and lighting replacement (April 28–29)
- **Lobby and common hallways** — fresh coat of paint on walls, touch-up of trim (April 30–May 1)

- The parking garage will be accessible at all times; however, please allow extra time when parking during garage work days
- There may be mild paint odour in common hallways on April 30–May 1; ensure your unit door is well sealed if sensitive to fumes

## Questions?

If you have any questions or concerns about the upcoming work, please contact building management in advance.
